5th International Manila Clam (Asari) Symposium

Date: 2–4 June, 2024

Kunsan National University, Korea

Marine Life Research Institute, Jeju National University in conjunction with Estuarine & Coastal Sciences Association (ECSA) and with colleagues in Korea, Japan and China will be holding an International Manila Clam Symposium in South Korea from 2nd to 4th June 2024. The event will take place at the Kunsan National University campus in Gunsan City and will include a field visit to Gomso Bay tidal flat and off-shore suspended Manila clam culture facility.
